Built with educators from the very first sketch.

Every decision we make is reviewed with teachers, students, and families who are helping us understand what a calmer planning flow can feel like. We're sharing progress notes, iterating openly, and keeping the product honest to real classroom rhythms.

Why now?

Teachers are stitching together slides, docs, and messaging tools. Akadeo pulls those pieces into a single canvas that saves time without forcing a new routine.

How we build

We prototype alongside small cohorts, document what lands, and ship updates weekly. If something doesn't help in practice, it doesn't stay in the product.

Principles guiding our launch

Start small

We'd rather do a few things with care than promise everything on day one.

Keep data clear

Every insight is human-readable. No black boxes—just context you can act on.

Respect energy

The interface is light, responsive, and tuned to work on modest hardware.
