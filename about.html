Built with educators, for educators.

Akadeo is the result of hundreds of conversations with teachers, students, and school leaders seeking a more human approach to digital learning.

Why we exist

We believe technology should amplify, not replace, the relationships that make learning meaningful. Akadeo exists to support educators in crafting joyful, inclusive classrooms.

Our mission

Deliver intuitive tools that help teachers understand every learner, respond in the moment, and celebrate growth together.

2021

Prototype built with a community of pioneering educators.

2022

2023

Launched analytics suite and collaboration hub.

Today

Trusted by forward-thinking districts and growing everyday.

Our compass

Values that guide every release.

Led by educators, technologists, and dreamers.

Akadeo’s core team includes former teachers, curriculum directors, product designers, and engineers from globally loved learning platforms. We’re united by the belief that delight and rigor can co-exist in every classroom experience.

  • 45% of our team has taught in K-12 classrooms
  • Collective experience across 18 countries and 12 languages
  • Advisory council of students and families shaping the roadmap

People centered

A multidisciplinary crew supporting every launch.
